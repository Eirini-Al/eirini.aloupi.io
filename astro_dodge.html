Astrogalactic Dodge

This game was created for and submitted to the GoedWare Game Jam #9.

The jam's theme was "broken physics"; based on that, we created a physics - based platformer, which emulates the look and feel of a space-themed pinball machine. The player can control the ball's gravity and movement through time, in order to progress through each of the four available levels.


Co - creators:

Simonian (Pixel Art - Animation), Menelaos Alexiou (Music & Sounds)
